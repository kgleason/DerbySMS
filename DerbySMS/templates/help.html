Gamblers

Here is a list of commands that you can text in order to play along.
Where you see words like {firstname}, you should put in a value for firstname, and drop the {}
  • Set your name: name {firstname} {lastname}
  • Place a bet: bet {shortname} {amount} (for example "bet h1 20")
  • Get your status: status
  • Get the status of a horse: status {shortname}
